A Special Ballad

✧

Upon a shore of violet sand,
Where silver waters meet the land,
I found a melody so rare,
Carried softly through the air.

No diamond bright nor morning star,
Could ever match just who you are.
With every breath and gentle word,
The sweetest song I've ever heard.

So let the seasons drift away,
In purple gold and lilac gray.
For in this heart, the truth is plain:
You are the sun behind the rain.

✨ 🎀 ☁️

Dedicated for Angela Nina Arceta

You are as rare as a purple rose
